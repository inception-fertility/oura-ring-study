Menstrual Cycle Digital Tracking Study

Oura and Inception Fertility and its family of brands, including The Prelude Network™, are partnering in a research study to observe the physiological changes throughout the menstrual cycle using the Oura Ring. The Oura Ring is a wearable device that combines advanced sensor technology with an easy-to-use mobile app to deliver precise, personalized health insights to the user.
